About Us

Lighting Services — A Division of Systems Plus Inc.

Lighting Services provides every kind of lighting solution possible for multi-residential buildings, industrial and commercial properties. We've been serving the Niagara Region and Greater Hamilton Area since 1990.

As we buy our lighting supplies and parts in bulk it allows us to pass on our economies of scale to you, the end user. Our repairs and maintenance services are priced to pass these savings on to our clients. We can replace existing lighting with energy efficient alternatives and can offer complete retrofits of your lighting systems — upgrading them to more efficient solutions.

Consultation in conjunction with our suppliers and manufacturers, allows you to have an informed decision on upgrading or replacement of your lighting systems. We offer various cost and energy savings solutions for you to choose from while always maintaining our pricing advantages.

What We Do

Our Lighting Services

"Lighting Services provides every kind of lighting solution possible for multi-residential buildings, industrial and commercial properties."

💡

New Lighting Fixtures

Supply and installation of new indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures for commercial, industrial, and multi-residential properties. Post top, canopy, wall pack, high bay, flat panel, and more.

🔌

New Lighting Poles

Installation of new lighting poles up to 30 feet, including accessories: crossarms, bullhorns, tenons, and adapters. Full supply and installation for parking lots and outdoor areas.

🔧

Repairs to Indoor/Outdoor Lighting

Reliable repairs to all types of indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures. From a single broken fitting to a full multi-building repair program — we handle it all.

☀️

Repairs to Sign Lighting

Experienced in diagnosing and repairing all types of sign lighting, including illuminated signs, channel letters, and outdoor business signage.

⚡

LED Retrofits & Conversions

We convert, retrofit or replace incandescent, fluorescent, compact fluorescent, high pressure sodium, and metal halide fixtures with energy-efficient LED. Some replacements may qualify for "Save On Energy" rebates.

📈

Energy Cost & Savings Solutions

Consultation in conjunction with our suppliers and manufacturers for informed decisions on upgrading your lighting. We offer various cost and energy savings solutions while always maintaining our pricing advantages.

⛽

Preventative Maintenance

Many of our clients prefer preventative maintenance for their lighting systems. Regular LED re-lamping prevents large overhaul costs and allows easier annual cost budgeting of your maintenance.

🔒

Outdoor Fixture Cleaning & Re-lamping

Light fixture maintenance and cleaning for building wall packs, walkway bollards and poles, and parking lot poles can return lighting levels to near original.

♻️

Used Lamp & Ballast Recycling

Responsible disposal and recycling of used lamps and ballasts. We handle the logistics so your property stays compliant and environmentally responsible.

📦

Supply of Lighting Products

Supply of lighting products for maintenance staff. No charge for delivery in the Niagara area (minimum order $100.00). LED, fluorescent, HPS, metal halide, and more.
